Welcome!

I will soon defend my PhD dissertation, supervised by Maria Aloni and Floris Roelofsen at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC) of the University of Amsterdam (UvA).

Starting from October 1st, I will start a postdoc at the ILLC under the supervision of Robert van Rooij.

My PhD project concerned formal semantics analyses of indefinite pronouns and determiners across languages. My approach was to develop formal semantics frameworks able to capture the complex landscape of indefinites cross-linguistically, integrating their distribution, functional restrictions, and diachronic pathways.
